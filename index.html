Household of Prayer International

Raising believers to walk in destiny through prayer, worship, and discipleship

Household of Prayer International is a Christ‑centered family devoted to raising men and women into the fulfillment of their God‑given purpose.

Rooted in prayer, worship, and discipleship, we equip believers to grow in faith and to impact their communities through intentional teaching and mentorship.

Through focused fellowship, practical outreach, and life‑changing teaching, we pursue transformation — bringing hope locally and to the nations.

Our vision is discovering and raising men in the fulfillment of their God given purpose.

Pastor Obed Agyapong

Founder • Visionary Leader

Pastor Obed leads with a passion for prayer, teaching, and discipleship. His leadership has inspired many to step into their God‑given purpose.

"Raising men to fulfil their divine purpose."
